The Studio

We believe
design should hurt
a little.

Vørm was founded in Berlin in 2019 with one idea: the safest creative choice is the most dangerous business decision. We work with founders, CMOs, and creative directors who already know what they want — they just need a team with the conviction to execute it without flinching.

Every project starts from identity. Not a logo, not a palette — a point of view. That point of view drives everything else: the motion language, the type system, the digital experience, and the campaign strategy.

Conviction over consensus
Craft at every scale
Speed without shortcuts
Work with us
How We Work

A process built for
fast-moving teams.

01

Discover

A focused two-day deep dive into your brand, competitors, and the whitespace nobody else is occupying. We leave with a brief that everyone commits to.

02

Define

Strategy before pixels. We map out a verbal identity, a creative direction, and a system architecture — all before we open a single design file.

03

Design

We present one direction, not three. The work is already resolved — not a mood board. Revisions sharpen, they don't restart.

04

Deliver

Production-ready files, motion assets, style guides, and a handoff call. You leave with a system, not just a set of exports.
